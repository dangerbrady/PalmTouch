- This kernel is only for the LG Nexus 5 (hammerhead)
- Flash TWRP using fastboot first 
- Then use TWRP to flash the kernel. Instructions: https://twrp.me/lg/lgnexus5.html

Capacitive images are written to /proc/capacitive_matrix. You can use libftsp.jar as described here [1] to access the capacitive images in your own Android application.

[1] https://github.com/interactionlab/InfiniTouch (see section Android Library)
